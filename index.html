About

Myself

Hi! This is Sonu Tiwari.


I am Sonu Tiwari, a dynamic and results-driven software engineer with a journey spanning across several prestigious organizations. I embarked on my professional journey with Infosys in 2018, where I honed my skills and gained a solid foundation in software development. Seeking new challenges, I transitioned to Deloitte in 2019, where I spent 1.5 years contributing my expertise to complex projects. Fuelled by a passion for innovation, I then fulfilled a personal aspiration by joining Dream11 as an SDE 1 front-end developer, where I contributed to crafting engaging user experiences. Following that, in 2021, I embraced an exciting opportunity at Swiggy as a Senior Front-End Engineer, leading a team and driving excellence in web development for over a year. Currently, I have found my stride at Uber, where I serve as an SDE 2 Front-End Engineer, leveraging my experience to create seamless and efficient user interfaces. My journey reflects my dedication to continuous growth and my commitment to delivering impactful solutions in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
